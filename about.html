About Ziqian

I am Zi Qian, an AI & Data Engineering student at Nanyang Polytechnic. While maintaining Director's List recognition across both semesters of AY2025/2026, I also worked part-time as a Sales Advisor at Takashimaya during holidays and non-exam periods — balancing real-world responsibility alongside my studies.

My journey has been shaped by consistent improvement, project-based learning, and a drive to apply what I learn to real problems.

I build practical systems across software, data engineering, IoT, and hardware prototyping — and I am always looking for the next problem worth solving.
