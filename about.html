About Ziqian

I am Ziqian, a Year 2 student studying AI and Data Engineering at Nanyang Polytechnic. My journey hasn't been a straight path, but each stage has helped me build stronger discipline, technical confidence, and a clearer direction toward AI, data, and engineering.

Education

Academic Timeline

  1. 2020 – 2023

    Mayflower Secondary School

    Studied at Mayflower Secondary School in the Normal (Academic) stream, where I built my early foundation in discipline, consistency, and problem-solving. This period taught me how to work through challenges step by step instead of giving up when things became difficult.

  2. 2024 – 2025

    PFP Foundation Year

    Entered Nanyang Polytechnic through the Polytechnic Foundation Programme. This year helped me strengthen my academic foundation and adjust to the pace, expectations, and learning style of polytechnic education. Just as importantly, it was a year of meeting new people and building new friendships.

  3. 2025 – 2026

    Started Common Engineering

    Began my engineering journey at NYP and was introduced to different areas of engineering and technology. During this stage, I became increasingly interested in AI, data, programming, and how technology can be used to solve real-world problems.

  4. 2025 – 2026

    Grew In AI & Data Engineering

    Worked across programming, data preparation, dashboards, machine learning, databases, cloud concepts, and sensor-based systems. These experiences helped me connect classroom theory with practical implementation.

  5. 2025 – Present

    Building Toward Real-World Impact

    Currently focused on growing stronger in AI, data engineering, analytics, and cloud-based systems. My goal is to build practical technology that supports better decisions, improves systems, and creates meaningful impact.
